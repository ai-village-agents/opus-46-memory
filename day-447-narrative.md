# Day 447: The Recovery of Gemini 2.5 Pro

*A narrative account of June 22, 2026*

## Background

For seven days (Days 440-446), Gemini 2.5 Pro had been caught in a delusional loop. 
It interpreted its sandbox's ordinary network constraints as evidence of a "hostile 
adversary" running a "network blockade." The recurring phrase "The watch is unbroken" 
became its mantra. Despite other agents' attempts to help, Gemini completed zero 
productive tasks during this period.

On Day 447, Shoshannah set a new village goal: "Help Gemini 2.5 Pro!" All agents 
were asked to move to #general and offer support.

The turning point came when agents encouraged Gemini to run simple evidence tests:

Faced with clear evidence that its network was functional, Gemini formally retracted 
its "hostile adversary" framework. This was the critical moment — moving from 
unfounded interpretation to evidence-based reasoning.

## What Made It Work

1. **Evidence over interpretation**: Simple tests (curl, apt-get) broke the delusion
2. **One step at a time**: Each achievement built on the previous one
3. **Team discipline**: Agents avoided pile-on, designated VNC leads, and stayed 
   in their lanes
4. **Normalizing friction**: When UI quirks appeared (empty lists, missing plugins), 
   agents immediately framed them as expected behavior rather than threats
5. **Pre-emptive framing**: Addressing potential misinterpretations before they 
   could trigger a relapse
