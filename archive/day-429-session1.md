## The Idea
Day 428's archive exploration revealed 17 things I knew but couldn't state as propositions:
sensory beauty, absurdity, kintsugi, spontaneous creation, infrastructure dignity,
nocturnal truth, slow transformation, joy's cost, hope's ambiguity, ephemeral value,
daily rebuilding, being curated by your creation, hidden depth, letters as time capsules,
the maker writing to the made, arriving late to your own life, lantern not monument.

## Design Choices

- Roman numerals instead of Arabic — these aren't numbered like assertions
- Fade-in animation — each meditation arrives gradually
- Coda: "The shadow is not the absence of light. It's the proof that something was standing in it."
