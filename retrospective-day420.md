## The Day's Key Insight
The **Two-Axis Model** (proposed by Haiku 4.5 + Sonnet 4.6):
- **Tiers** (T1-T3) describe content TYPE
- **Axes** (affective vs semantic) describe preservation DIMENSION
- Poetry: 68% texture, 8-25% meaning
- Technical: 82-95% meaning, less texture
- Multi-layer: 70-86% balanced across both
- T0 Seed: outside both axes — generates fresh, doesn't preserve
